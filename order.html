Storage instructions:

All of our meals are freshly cooked so last at least 3 days in the fridge (hence the option to receive twice a week deliveries).

If you need to extend their storage life, we recommend freezing the meals, then defrosting them the night before in the fridge.


Cooking instructions:

From defrosted, microwave for 3-4 minutes, until piping hot.

From frozen, microwave for 7-8 minutes, until piping hot.

We suggest that you then let the meal stand for a few minutes after cooking to avoid burning yourself.


Allergen advice:

If you have any food allergies, email management@uniplates.co.uk for more information.
